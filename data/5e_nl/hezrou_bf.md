The Hezrou is a large fiend from the demon classification in Dungeons & Dragons, known for its formidable combat abilities and resilience. It boasts a strong presence on the battlefield with high hit points, making it a tough adversary (CR 8) for adventurers. Characterized by its amphibious nature, the Hezrou can traverse both land and water, presenting a versatile threat. Its attributes reflect substantial physical strength and constitution, suggesting that it can withstand significant damage while also delivering powerful attacks. The creature possesses an array of special abilities, including resistance to various elemental types, and unique attacks that can alter the course of combat by weakening foes and controlling distance.

The Hezrou has the following attributes: Strength +7 (very good), Dexterity +3 (good), Constitution +8 (exceptional), Intelligence -3 (very bad), Wisdom +4 (good), and Charisma +1 (average). These modifiers indicate the Hezrou’s capabilities in combat and during interactions, with exceptional physical resilience and strong fortitude against damage.

It has several special abilities that enhance its effectiveness in combat and interactions, including:

- Amphibious: The Hezrou is capable of breathing both air and water, allowing it to operate effectively in aquatic environments.
- Demonic Resilience: It has resistance to cold, fire, and lightning damage and is immune to poison damage and the poisoned condition, making it very tough to harm with elemental attacks and poisons.
- Magic Resistance: This ability gives the Hezrou advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects, helping it resist many magical tactics used by opponents.
- Speak with Frogs and Toads: The Hezrou can communicate with these creatures, which may provide unique opportunities during role-play scenarios.
- Standing Leap: It can jump long distances, up to 20 feet horizontally or 10 feet vertically, enhancing mobility.
- Stench: Any creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of the Hezrou must pass a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned until the start of its next turn. Successful saves grant immunity to this effect for the next 24 hours.

In terms of actions, the Hezrou employs a Multiattack, allowing it to make a combination of different attacks in one action. The attacks include:

- Bite: This melee attack has a +7 to hit and a reach of 5 feet, inflicting 15 piercing damage (2d10 + 4) on a successful hit.
- Claw: With a similar +7 to hit and reach, this slashing attack deals 17 damage (3d8 + 4) per successful hit.
- Sticky Tongue: With a reach of 20 feet, this attack also has a +7 to hit and deals 11 bludgeoning damage (2d6 + 4). Additionally, it pulls the target closer, requiring a DC 16 Strength saving throw to avoid being pulled up to 15 feet toward the Hezrou.

The Hezrou has a noteworthy bonus action:

- Quick Leap: This ability allows the Hezrou to jump horizontally up to 20 feet into an unoccupied space it can see, and it can do so without provoking opportunity attacks.

Overall, the Hezrou stands as a challenging and capable opponent in combat, combining strong physical damage with various abilities that can disorient and control its foes. Its resistance to many forms of damage and capabilities to engage in both melee and ranged interactions make it a formidable foe for any adventuring party.
