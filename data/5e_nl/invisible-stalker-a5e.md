The Invisible Stalker is a Medium Elemental creature known for its stealth and tracking abilities, making it a formidable presence on the battlefield. With an Armor Class of 14 and a substantial pool of Hit Points, totaling 104, it can withstand a fair amount of damage while remaining elusive. Its Challenge Rating is 6, suggesting it poses a moderate threat to adventurers, offering 2,300 XP upon defeat. The Invisible Stalker possesses high Dexterity and Strength, with Dexterity at 18 (+4), making its stealth capabilities exceptional, and Strength at 16 (+3) allows for decent melee damage. While it is immune to poison and resistant to damage from nonmagical weapons, it excels in conditions that grant it even greater survivability. The Stalker can easily navigate both the physical and mystical realms due to its elemental nature, which allows it to move through the world without needing air or rest. 

The attributes of the Invisible Stalker are as follows: Strength is 16 with a modifier of +3, resulting in good melee capabilities; Dexterity is 18 with a modifier of +4, providing an exceptional stealth bonus; Constitution is 14 with a +2 modifier, giving it average durability; Intelligence is 10 with a 0 modifier, indicating average cognitive ability; Wisdom is 14 with a +2 modifier, translating to good perception; and Charisma is 10 with a 0 modifier reflecting average social interaction skills. 

For skills, it has a Perception score of +5 and a Stealth score of +7; both are very good, allowing the Invisible Stalker to detect threats while remaining hidden effectively. It is immune to several conditions such as fatigue, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained, and unconscious, ensuring it remains a constant threat regardless of enemy actions. The Stalker possesses darkvision with a range of 60 feet, allowing it to see in darkness, and has a passive Perception score of 15. The languages it understands include Auran and Common. 

The special abilities of the Invisible Stalker further enhance its mechanics. Its Invisibility makes it undetectable to most creatures, appearing only as a vague outline to those who can see invisible beings. The Wind Tracker ability allows the Stalker to know the direction and distance to a designated quarry, as long as it is on the same plane and not separated by barriers that prevent airflow. Lastly, its Elemental Nature means it does not need air, sustenance, or sleep, making it an enduring opponent. 

In terms of actions, the Invisible Stalker can perform a Multiattack, allowing it to make two melee attacks in one turn. Each Slam attack has a +6 to hit with a reach of 5 feet, inflicting 12 (2d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage upon a hit. On a critical hit, the target is not only damaged but also pushed up to 15 feet and knocked prone, showcasing the Stalker’s potential to disrupt enemy formations. 

Additionally, the Stalker has a bonus action called Gust with a recharge mechanic of 6, which enables it to briefly transform into a gust of wind. This ability allows it to move up to its Speed without provoking opportunity attacks and pass through openings as narrow as 1 inch without squeezing, facilitating its evasive capabilities and enhancing its ability to navigate around foes seamlessly.
