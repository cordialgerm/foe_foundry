Mimic is skilled in: High Strength score allows effective grappling and close combat attacks; impressive ambush tactics with the ability to adhere to and restrain its victims. Mimic is skilled in: Stealth proficiency allows it to remain hidden effectively while waiting to ambush. Mimic has the following most powerful ability: Adhesive ability allows the Mimic to trap and grapple targets that come in contact with it. Mimic can unleash: Utilizes pseudopod attacks to grapple foes and a bite attack that inflicts both piercing and acid damage on restrained targets. Mimic is weak to: Low Intelligence and Charisma diminish its strategic capabilities and social interactions; its reliance on ambush makes it vulnerable to detection.
