Interesting facts about Chimera: Has the Reactive Heads ability, allowing it to take multiple reactions. Bickering Heads ability can distract it during combat by appealing to its various heads. Resistant to conditions like being blinded, charmed, and frightened. Can make multiple attacks in a single turn thanks to the Multiattack feature. Possesses a powerful Tail Swipe reaction for retaliating against melee attackers.
