Ice Devil is strong in: High resilience against cold and ice-based attacks, immunity to fire and poison, and the ability to manipulate the battlefield with ice and snow. Ice Devil is skilled in: Very perceptive with a passive perception score of 13, aiding in awareness during encounters. Ice Devil can use: Ice Wall ability creates barriers that control the battlefield and inflict heavy cold damage. Ice Devil can perform: Employs a Multiattack feature with powerful bite, claws, and tail strikes, delivering piercing and cold damage. Ice Devil has the following weaknesses: Vulnerable to fire damage and can be outsmarted by clever opponents exploiting its strategic tendencies.
