Ankheg is described as: The Ankheg is a large, insect-like creature with a segmented body covered in tough chitinous armor. It has massive, sharp mandibles capable of crushing rock and bone. Its multiple legs enable it to burrow through the earth at alarming speeds. Ankheg is memorable for: The Ankheg's ability to ambush prey from underground and its vicious acid spray attack make it a terrifying encounter.
