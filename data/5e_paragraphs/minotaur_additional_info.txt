Useful tidbits about Minotaur: Can increase damage output during a Bleeding Frenzy when low on hit points. Has Reckless ability, allowing for advantage on melee attacks at a risk of being targeted easier. Doubles damage to structures as a Siege Monster, making it destructive in hostile environments. Capable of dealing extra damage if it charges before a Gore attack. Resistance to exhaustion and fear, providing tactical advantages in chaotic encounters.
