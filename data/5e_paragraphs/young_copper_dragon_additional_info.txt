Useful tidbits about Young Copper Dragon: Possesses a special ability called Corrosive Scales that damages metal weapons striking it. Can change shape into any beast or humanoid of equal or lower challenge rating. Has a unique ability called Flow Within the Mountain for enhanced stealth in mountainous regions. Innate spellcasting allows casting of suggestion and hideous laughter three times per day. Has a passive perception of 17, making it highly aware of its surroundings.
