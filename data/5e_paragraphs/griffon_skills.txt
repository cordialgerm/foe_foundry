Griffon is skilled in: Exceptional mobility both on the ground and in the air, strong physical presence, and advantage on attack rolls when allies are nearby. Griffon is proficient in: Possesses proficient Perception skills, giving it a high capability to notice hidden objects or creatures. Griffon can execute: Pounce allows rapid strikes, knocking foes prone and capitalizing on their vulnerability. Griffon can unleash: Multiattack includes a Beak attack and a Claws attack, dealing significant damage. Griffon has the following weaknesses: Relatively low Armor Class and lacks magical abilities.
