Mammoth has the following strengths: High damage output from melee attacks, capable of knocking opponents prone, and can control the battlefield effectively. Mammoth has the following skills: None notable. Mammoth can use: Trampling Charge, allowing it to knock enemies prone and follow-up with a Stomp attack as a bonus action. Mammoth can unleash: Specializes in powerful Gore and Stomp attacks, able to knock enemies prone and follow up with additional damage. Mammoth is susceptible to: Low Dexterity score results in difficulty with agility and evasion, leading to challenges in avoiding area effects.
