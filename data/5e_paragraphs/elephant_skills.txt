Elephant has the following strengths: High strength allows for powerful melee attacks and ability to absorb damage. Elephant can perform: Trampling Charge allows it to knock down opponents and follow up with stomping attacks. Elephant can unleash: Capable of gore attacks with tusks inflicting piercing damage, and stomping on prone creatures for additional bludgeoning damage. Elephant is weak to: Low dexterity leads to poor agility and vulnerability to quick opponents.
