Barbed Devil has the following strengths: Immunities to fire and poison damage, resistance to cold damage, and the ability to grapple effectively. Barbed Devil boasts: Skilled in Athletics, Deception, Insight, and Perception, showcasing its versatility. Barbed Devil can execute: Barbed Hide inflicts piercing damage to creatures grappling or grappled by the devil at the start of its turn. Barbed Devil has the following attacks: Can perform two claw attacks and one tail attack or cast 'Hurl Flame' twice, dealing piercing and fire damage. Barbed Devil has the following weaknesses: Vulnerable to radiant damage and has a reliance on maintaining grapple for control.
