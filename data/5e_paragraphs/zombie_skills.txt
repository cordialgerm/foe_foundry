Zombie has the following strengths: Immune to poison damage and the poisoned condition, and can potentially survive lethal damage with Undead Fortitude. Zombie boasts: Passive perception is low, making it somewhat oblivious to its surroundings. Zombie can use: Undead Fortitude allows the Zombie to avoid falling unconscious when taking lethal damage. Zombie can unleash: Can perform a Grab or Slam attack to restrain and damage opponents. Zombie is weak to: Lacks mobility, low Intelligence means predictable behavior.
