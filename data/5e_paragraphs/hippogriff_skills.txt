Hippogriff has the following strengths: Exceptional mobility with flying speed, strong physical attacks, and heightened senses. Hippogriff is skilled in: Proficient in Perception with a total bonus of +5, granting it excellent awareness of surroundings. Hippogriff has the following most powerful ability: Heightened Sight increases its Perception score to 18 when relying on sight. Hippogriff can use: Can use Multiattack to strike with beak and claws, dealing piercing and slashing damage respectively. Hippogriff has the following weaknesses: Low intelligence and charisma make it unsuitable for complex tasks and social interaction.
