Did you know that Ogre: Ogres are known for their simple-mindedness, making them easily fooled or manipulated. They can perform a Shoving Rush, knocking targets prone while avoiding opportunity attacks. Ogres resist being stunned, making them more challenging to incapacitate. They often wield improvised weapons in addition to their Greatclub. Ogres can engage in a Multiattack, allowing them to make two actions per turn.
