Useful tidbits about Black Pudding: Can climb difficult surfaces, including ceilings, without difficulty. Splits into two smaller puddings when taking lightning or slashing damage if above 10 hit points, increasing its threat level. Does not require air or sleep, remaining active and ready for encounters without rest. Can reduce the radius of nearby light sources within 60 feet, creating tactical advantages in dimly lit environments. Dissolves 2-inch-thick nonmagical wood or metal in one round, showcasing its formidable corrosive capabilities.
