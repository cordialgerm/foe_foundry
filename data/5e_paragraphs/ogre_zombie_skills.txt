Ogre Zombie has the following strengths: High strength and durability with immunity to poison and exhausting conditions. Ogre Zombie can unleash: Undead Fortitude allows it to potentially drop to 1 hit point instead of 0 when reduced. Ogre Zombie can perform: Employs powerful melee strikes with a Greatclub, and can inflict damage with additional attacks like Bite or grab foes. Ogre Zombie is not good at: Low Dexterity and poor Intelligence, making it vulnerable to finesse attacks.
