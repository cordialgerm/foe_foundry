Sea Hag has the following strengths: Skilled in illusion and enchantment magic, strong in physical attacks with high Strength and Constitution. Sea Hag is capable of: Proficient in Deception and Insight, enhancing its capabilities for manipulation and understanding others. Sea Hag can unleash: Death Glare can reduce frightened targets to 0 hit points, creating a significant threat in battle. Sea Hag can magically unleash: Innate Spellcasting allows it to cast spells like Dancing Lights and Disguise Self at will. Sea Hag can execute: Strikes with claws that deal slashing damage and uses a deadly gaze attack to frighten or incapacitate foes. Sea Hag is susceptible to: Moderate challenge rating and average Dexterity makes it vulnerable to coordinated attacks.
