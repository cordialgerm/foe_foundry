Red Dragon Wyrmling has the following strengths: Immunity to fire damage and capabilities in both melee and area-of-effect fire attacks with high damage potential. Red Dragon Wyrmling has the following skills: Excels in intimidation, making its threats very imposing with a +4 modifier. Red Dragon Wyrmling has the following most powerful ability: Fire Breath, allowing it to unleash a devastating cone of fire in combat, inflicting massive damage to multiple targets. Red Dragon Wyrmling can execute: Bite attack dealing significant piercing damage plus fire damage, and a powerful Fire Breath exhaling a cone of fire that causes high damage. Red Dragon Wyrmling is susceptible to: Average Dexterity reduces effectiveness in stealth and ranged combat.
