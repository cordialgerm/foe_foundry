Troll has the following strengths: Exceptional Constitution and the ability to regenerate lost hit points at the start of their turn. Troll is proficient in: Skilled in stealth, allowing them to remain undetected in their environment. Troll can perform: Regeneration that allows the troll to recover 10 hit points at the start of its turn. Troll can execute: Multiattack with a bite and claw, allowing them to inflict significant damage in combat. Troll is susceptible to: Vulnerable to fire and acid damage, which impede their regeneration.
