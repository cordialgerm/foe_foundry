Interesting facts about Otyugh: Exhibits Aberrant Resilience, resistant to being charmed, frightened, paralyzed, or stunned. Communicates using Limited Telepathy to convey simple messages and images to nearby allies. Possesses a Stench ability that can poison nearby creatures. Can use Tentacle Slam to deal bludgeoning damage and stun grappled foes. The Bite attack has a significant risk of inflicting a disease that reduces hit point maximum.
