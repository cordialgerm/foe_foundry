Here are some interesting facts about Wereboar: Can shift seamlessly between humanoid, hybrid, and boar forms. Possesses a Relentless ability that prevents it from being reduced to 0 hit points once per day. Affected by wolfsbane, which poisons it and can harm it like a silvered weapon. Excels in melee combat with multiattack options. Cursed lycanthropy can be passed on to humanoids through successful tusk attacks.
