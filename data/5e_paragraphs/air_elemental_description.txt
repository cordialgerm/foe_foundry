Air Elemental is referred to as: An Air Elemental is a swirling, translucent mass of wind and vapor, capable of generating powerful gusts and whirlwinds. Its form may condense into a vaguely humanoid shape with swirling arms and legs made of pure air. Air Elemental is remarkable for: Its ability to suffocate foes by creating a vortex of air and move through narrow spaces makes it a challenging opponent.
