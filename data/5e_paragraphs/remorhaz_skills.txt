Remorhaz is skilled in: High Strength and Constitution scores, immunity to cold and fire damage, can grapple multiple creatures at once. Remorhaz boasts: Lacks notable skill proficiencies, limiting its effectiveness outside combat scenarios. Remorhaz can unleash: Heated Body causes fire damage to anyone grappling or hitting it in close range, increasing if bloodied.
