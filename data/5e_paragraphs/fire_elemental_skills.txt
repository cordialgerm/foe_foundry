Fire Elemental is skilled in: Immune to fire and poison damage; possesses high mobility and area damage abilities. Fire Elemental can unleash: Fiery Aura that damages nearby creatures and causes ongoing fire damage to those it ignites. Fire Elemental can execute: Multiattack with two slam attacks that deal fire damage and can ignite targets; also has powerful ranged fire attacks. Fire Elemental has the following weaknesses: Vulnerable to cold damage; takes damage if submerged in water.
