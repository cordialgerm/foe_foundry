Basilisk has the following strengths: Possesses a powerful petrifying gaze that can immobilize foes, along with venomous bite attacks. Basilisk can use: Petrifying Gaze, which can turn enemies to stone and remove them from combat. Basilisk can unleash: Utilizes a deadly Stone Gaze to petrify and a venomous bite that combines piercing and poison damage. Basilisk is weak to: Low speed and poor Dexterity make it vulnerable to ranged attacks.
