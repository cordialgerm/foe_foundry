Useful tidbits about Bearded Devil: Has a multiattack option capable of making two Beard Spines attacks and one Glaive attack in one turn. The poisoned condition from its beard prevents affected targets from regaining hit points. Can enhance the pain of enemies, incapacitating them if they fail a saving throw. Known for its cruel enjoyment of battle and causing suffering. Effective in ambush tactics, leveraging its darkvision and mobility.
