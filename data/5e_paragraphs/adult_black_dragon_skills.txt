Adult Black Dragon has the following strengths: Immune to acid damage, high Armor Class, extensive Hit Points, and a variety of powerful attacks. Adult Black Dragon is good at: Excels in stealth and perception, regularly ambushing foes with high efficiency. Adult Black Dragon can use: The Acid Breath can obliterate foes in a wide line, causing massive acid damage and potential blindness. Adult Black Dragon can unleash: Can bite, claw, and strike with its tail, using a combination of piercing, slashing, and bludgeoning damage, along with acid damage from its breath and other attacks. Adult Black Dragon is weak to: Reliance on saving throws for certain abilities, can be temporarily weakened by its Legendary Resistance.
