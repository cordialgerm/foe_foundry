Tarrasque has the following description: The Tarrasque is a towering monstrosity, standing over 50 feet tall with a massive, armored body, claws capable of tearing through stone, and a maw large enough to swallow buildings whole. Its appearance is imposing and destructive, leaving devastation in its wake. Tarrasque is noteworthy for: Considered one of the most powerful creatures, it regenerates health and is nearly impossible to kill, making it a true behemoth of destruction.
