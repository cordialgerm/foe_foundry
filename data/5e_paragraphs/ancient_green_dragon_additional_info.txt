Interesting facts about Ancient Green Dragon: Amphibious, can breathe both air and water. Legendary Resistance allows it to ignore failed saving throws a limited number of times each day. Woodland Stalker ability gives advantages on stealth checks in forested areas. Blood Toxicity punishes melee attackers with poison damage when the dragon is bloodied. Hallucinogenic Gas affects creatures around it post-Poison Breath, causing fear and hallucinations.
