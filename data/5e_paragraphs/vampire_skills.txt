Vampire is good at: High Dexterity and Charisma, exceptional stealth abilities, and powerful regeneration. Vampire is capable of: Skilled in Stealth and Persuasion, making it adept at evading detection and manipulating others. Vampire has the following most powerful ability: Regeneration: Heals 20 hit points at the start of its turn, unless damaged by radiant sources. Vampire has the following attacks: Utilizes sharp fangs to deliver lethal bites and grapples to control victims, draining their life force. Vampire is weak to: Vulnerable to sunlight and running water, which can deactivate its formidable abilities.
