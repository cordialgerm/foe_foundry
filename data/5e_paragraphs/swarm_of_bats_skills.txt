Swarm of Bats is strong in: High mobility and the ability to occupy the same space as other creatures, making them effective in tight quarters. Swarm of Bats is skilled in: Has proficiency in Perception checks, particularly those that rely on hearing. Swarm of Bats has the following most powerful ability: Echolocation allows them to navigate and attack even in complete darkness, although it is hindered when deafened. Swarm of Bats can execute: Uses Bites to attack, capable of significant damage when striking in numbers. Swarm of Bats is vulnerable to: Very low Strength makes them ineffective in physical confrontations, relying instead on numbers and chaos.
