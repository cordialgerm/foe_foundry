Thug has the following strengths: High melee damage output, strong intimidation skills, and ability to incapacitate foes. Thug boasts: Possesses proficiency in intimidation, aiding in coercive social interactions. Thug can perform: Brutal ability allows melee attacks to deal an extra die of damage, significantly enhancing threat level. Thug can execute: Uses multiattack with brass knuckles for increased damage potential and has a heavy crossbow for ranged options. Thug is weak to: Limited versatility outside of combat and vulnerable to ranged attacks.
