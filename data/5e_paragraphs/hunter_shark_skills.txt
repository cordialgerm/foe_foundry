Hunter Shark is good at: High strength and speed make it an efficient predator in water. Hunter Shark is proficient in: Possesses a perception skill bonus of +2 to notice changes in its aquatic environment. Hunter Shark has the following most powerful ability: Blood Frenzy provides advantage on attacks against creatures that are not at full health. Hunter Shark has the following attacks: Powerful Bite attack that inflicts 11 piercing damage and allows for an additional bite if it hits its target. Hunter Shark is weak to: Ineffective on land due to a swimming-only movement capability. Low intelligence and charisma affect its strategic abilities.
