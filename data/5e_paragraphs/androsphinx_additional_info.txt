Useful tidbits about Androsphinx: Possesses resistance to exhaustion and cannot be frightened. Can unleash a powerful roar that induces fear or deals damage to those who fail their saving throws. Ability to teleport, adding to its unpredictability in combat. Induces effects such as paralysis or deafness with its roars. Can cast spells without needing material components, granting it versatility in combat.
