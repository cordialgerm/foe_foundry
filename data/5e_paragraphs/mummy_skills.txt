Mummy is strong in: High resilience against many forms of damage, particularly nonmagical attacks, and immunity to necrotic damage. Mummy is skilled in: Resistant to many conditions, showcasing exceptional durability against effects like charm and paralysis. Mummy can execute: The Mummy Rot curse, which prevents healing and reduces the hit point maximum over time, potentially leading to death. Mummy can perform: Able to execute a multiattack with a Dreadful Glare to frighten or paralyze, followed by a powerful Rotting Fist attack that deals both bludgeoning and necrotic damage. Mummy has the following weaknesses: Low Dexterity makes it vulnerable to ranged attacks and quick opponents.
