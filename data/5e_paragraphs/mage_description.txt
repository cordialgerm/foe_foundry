Mage is known as: A Mage wears robes adorned with mystical symbols and carries a staff, wand, or other arcane foci. They are highly intelligent beings dedicated to the study of arcane magic. Mage is noteworthy for: The Mage excels in spellcasting, using devastating spells like fireball and cone of cold, and possesses the ability to teleport using dimension door and misty step.
