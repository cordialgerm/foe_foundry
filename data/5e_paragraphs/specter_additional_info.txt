Here are some interesting facts about Specter: Incorporeal Movement allows it to move through creatures and objects. Sunlight Sensitivity imposes disadvantage on attack rolls and perception checks in sunlight. It doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep, enhancing its ghostly endurance. The Retributive Shriek ability can frighten attackers, imposing psychological stress. It has resistances to several damage types, enhancing survival in combat.
