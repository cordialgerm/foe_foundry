Wereboar is proficient in: High damage resistance to non-magical weapons and the ability to shapeshift provides both offensive and defensive advantages. Wereboar is capable of: Noted for its good melee combat skills and ability to frighten opponents with Challenging Snort. Wereboar can perform: Charge ability that allows extra damage and the potential to knock targets prone. Wereboar can execute: Melee attacks utilizing tusks and powerful mauls, capable of knocking prone and inflicting lycanthropy. Wereboar is vulnerable to: Low armor class in humanoid form makes it vulnerable to ranged attacks; average Dexterity reduces evasiveness.
