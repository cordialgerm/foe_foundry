Phase Spider has the following strengths: Highly mobile and stealthy, capable of ambushing and evading attacks through ethereal travel. Phase Spider has the following skills: Skilled in Stealth with a notable +6 bonus, allowing it to remain hidden effectively. Phase Spider can unleash: Ethereal Jaunt allows it to shift between the Material and Ethereal Planes, enhancing its ambush and escape tactics. Phase Spider can use: Uses a venomous bite that can paralyze targets and a multiattack that includes sharp barbed legs. Phase Spider has the following weaknesses: Low intelligence and charisma, making it less strategic in encounters.
