Useful tidbits about Ancient Silver Dragon: Can use Cloud Strider to avoid harmful effects at high altitudes and become ephemeral clouds. Has legendary actions for enhanced tactical versatility, including options to frighten enemies and reposition. Can use Change Shape to transform into a humanoid or beast, retaining many of its abilities. Legendary Resistance allows it to succeed on saving throws it would otherwise fail. Its potent Frightful Presence can instill fear in nearby creatures, adding a psychological tactic to its combat style.
