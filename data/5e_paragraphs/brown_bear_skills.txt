Brown Bear has the following strengths: Strong physical attacks with high damage output; excellent swimmer and climber. Brown Bear is good at: Proficient in Perception checks, enhancing its ability to detect prey. Brown Bear can execute: Keen Smell, granting advantage on Perception checks relying on smell, making it difficult to surprise. Brown Bear can use: Can deliver a powerful bite (11 piercing damage) and swiping claws attack (9 slashing damage), capable of grappling smaller targets. Brown Bear has the following weaknesses: Low armor class makes it vulnerable to physical attacks; limited intelligence and strategy.
