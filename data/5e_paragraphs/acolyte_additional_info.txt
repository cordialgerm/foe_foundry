Interesting facts about Acolyte: Acolytes serve as assistants in religious ceremonies and rituals. They have a spell save DC of 12 and a +4 bonus to spell attacks. Abilities allow them to cast spells like Bless and Cure Wounds multiple times a day. The Acolyte's role becomes critical in maintaining party survivability during conflicts. Typically function as a healer and support character in adventuring parties.
