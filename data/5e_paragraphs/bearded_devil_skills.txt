Bearded Devil is proficient in: Immunity to fire and poison damage, resistant to cold damage, and possesses Magic Resistance against spells. Bearded Devil is proficient in: Noteworthy saving throws in Strength, Constitution, and Wisdom, making it effective in physical confrontations. Bearded Devil can use: Its beard attack can poison enemies, preventing healing, while the glaive creates an infernal wound that deals ongoing damage. Bearded Devil can unleash: Utilizes multiattack to make strikes with its writhing beard and glaive, inflicting piercing and slashing damage along with powerful poison effects. Bearded Devil has the following weaknesses: Lower Intelligence and Charisma make it less effective in social situations and strategic spellcasting.
