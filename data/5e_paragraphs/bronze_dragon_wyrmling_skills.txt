Bronze Dragon Wyrmling has the following strengths: Immunity to lightning damage, possesses significant mobility in land and water, and effective area damage potential with its breath weapons. Bronze Dragon Wyrmling boasts: It has proficiency in Perception and Stealth, enhancing its abilities to notice hidden threats and navigate stealthily. Bronze Dragon Wyrmling can use: Lightning Breath allows it to unleash a potent attack in a line, dealing significant area damage. Bronze Dragon Wyrmling can unleash: Makes a Bite attack dealing piercing damage and can execute breath attacks that inflict lightning damage or push foes away. Bronze Dragon Wyrmling is not good at: Average Dexterity and lower intelligence compared to older dragons make it less nimble and clever.
