Giant Hyena is proficient in: High movement speed and powerful bite attacks; excels in melee combat and coordination with pack tactics. Giant Hyena is good at: Has proficiency in Perception, granting it good awareness of its surroundings. Giant Hyena can execute: Bite attack that deals significant piercing damage, with the ability to take down multiple enemies in a single turn. Giant Hyena is not good at: Low intelligence and charisma, limiting its strategic thinking and social interactions.
