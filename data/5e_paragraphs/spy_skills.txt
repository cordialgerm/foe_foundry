Spy has the following strengths: Expertise in stealth and deception, high Dexterity for effective ranged and melee attacks. Spy has the following skills: Proficient in deception, insight, investigation, perception, persuasion, sleight of hand, and stealth, with strong modifiers in key areas. Spy can use: Sneak Attack, allowing them to deal significant extra damage under certain conditions. Spy can unleash: Utilizes shortswords for melee strikes and hand crossbows for ranged attacks, both capable of inflicting piercing damage. Spy has the following weaknesses: Average physical and mental attributes limit durability in direct combat.
