Unique Monsters with Handcrafted Powers

Every monster draws from a vault of 500+ handcrafted powers. Here are a few examples:

Adhesive

The aboleth adheres to anything that touches it (including weapons). A Huge or smaller creature or object adhered to the aboleth is also Grappled by it (escape DC 14). Ability checks made to escape this grapple have disadvantage.

Modify Memory

Immediately after hitting with an attack, the aboleth attempts to reshape that creature's memories. The creature must succeed on a DC 12 Intelligence saving throw or be affected as if by the Modify Memory spell. A creature that succeeds on the save is immune to this effect for 24 hours.

Death Ward

When a creature within 30 feet of the priest takes damage that would reduce it to zero hit points, the priest can use its reaction to instead set that creature's hit points to 10.

Heroism

The priest inspires another friendly creature within 60 ft, granting it 10 temporary hit points. While those temporary hitpoints are active, the creature has advantage on saving throws and is immune to being frightened or charmed.
